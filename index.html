Kepl

Telescopic & Guyed Masts

Kepl backed by its R&D and state of the art infrastructure Designs and Manufactures Customized Pneumatic Telescopic Mast which are ideal for communications, surveillance, elevated testing and mobile radar applications. Locking models are available when mast deployment is needed for extended periods. Ease in operation, quick deployment and transportation are the key differentiators of these masts when compared to other types.

Kepl

Counterpoise Earth System

Kepl has designed and manufactured counterpoise earth systems for DVOR used as navigational aids in the Airports. We have supplied several systems to Airport Authority of India and exported to Chile, South America, Bangladesh, UAE, Qatar, and Sharjah etc.

Antenna System

Kepl develop and manufactures MF,HF,VHF and UHF antennas to cater defence, PSUs and some private organisation requirements. Basing on its core strength in antenna products Kepl has expanded its antenna product range with the manufacture of Base station and Microwave antennas used in the cellular networks.

About Us

Welcome to Kepl

Kepl with its inception in 1964 has professional experience of over five (05) decades. Combining our intensive in-house design and strong focus on the manufacturing process, Kepl has become the market leader in India producing Steel Structures, Antenna systems, Pneumatic Telescopic Masts, Self Supporting and Guyed Towers, Counterpoise Earth Systems, Diesel Generators, etc. Kepl with its several manufacturing and Service divisions has the man power exceeding 1000 employees.
