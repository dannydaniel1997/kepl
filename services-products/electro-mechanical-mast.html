Electro Mechanical Telescopic Mast

The electromechanical telescopic mast is made of high tensile aluminum alloy sections. The sections are extended or retracted by a screw rod, driven by single phase electric motor. It can also be operated manually. The mast is provided with "Auto Lock and Unlock System&. These masts are offered with extended heights from 3 to 15 Mtrs with self supporting and guy supporting options.

Features:

  • The mast can be remotely operated by an operator sitting in his cabin.
  • The mast automatically stops at maximum extended height/min retracted height.
  • Any section can be locked, in extended or retracted position as and when required.
  • The mast can be manually rotated 360 degrees in "Azimuth".
  • Both Vehicle as well as ground mounted versions are available.
  • Smooth and noiseless operation during extension and retraction.
  • Reliable operation and easy maintenance.
  • Quick and easy to deploy.
  • Can withstand wind loads up to 120 kmph.
  • Can carry head loads up to 100kgs (Depends upon requirements).
  • Long life span with minimum maintenance.
  • Provide both self support " guy support.
  • Extremely reliable in all weather environment conditions.
  • Extremely safe operation.

The key advantages are:

  • Convenient ground and vehicle mounting options
  • Ease of operation
  • Extremely reliable in all weather conditions as per JSS55555 environment
  • Manual rotation or stationary – depending on the need
  • Extremely safe operation
  • High quality design
  • Corrosion resistant parts
