Antenna Systems Division

Kadevi has in-house full scale R & D centre with an experience and proven capability and the necessary infrastructure to design, manufacture, test, install and commission the antennas to customers specific requirements. We have developed indigenously with the help of DLRL several antennas in the frequency band of MF, HF, VHF, UHF catering essentially to defence, PSUs and few private organisation requirements which include Yagi array, Telemetry, Sleeve Monopole, Omni Magnetic Base, Bicone, Discone, Whip, Conifan, GP antennae.

We have received awards for import substitution of antenna products from defence, ELCINA and AP State Government.

Kadevi developed Omni direction whip antennas in the frequency band 824 to 896MHz, for Tata Tele Services for their WLL project and supplied 15,000 whip antennas for their wireless telephone booths supported by CDMA technology in the year 2002.

Basing on its core strength in antenna products Kadevi has expanded its antenna product range with the manufacture of Base station and Microwave antennas used in the cellular networks.

Kadevi has setup full-fledged manufacturing and testing facility for BTS and Microwave antennas required by mobile service providers such as VodaFone, Indus, Tata Tele, BSNL, etc. we have technical association with a renowned Chinese group for developing state of art produ cts in BTS and Microwave antennas.

KEPL has full scale R & D centre and all the necessary infrastructure to design, manufacture, test, install and commission the antennas to customers specific requirements.

The range of antennas in these categories comprises:

Base Station Panel antennas:

  • Single band 900 Mhz or 1800 Mhz GSM and CDMA antennas
  • Dual band GSM/ CDMA antennas
  • Multi port antennas

Microwave Antennas:

  • 0.3m, 0.6m and 1.2m diameter Standard, High and Ultra -High Performance antennas in the frequency range of 4.4GHz to 26.5 GHz.
